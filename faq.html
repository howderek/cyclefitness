Do you repair bikes?

Yes, our shop offers bicycle repair services! Repairs start at $50 plus parts.

Is this a fitness program?

No, but we are certainly fitness enthusiasts, and you might be interested in joining us on our bike rides as a great way to stay in shape.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy if you are not satisfied with your bike.

Is it possible to special order bikes?

Absolutely! We'll help you get the bicycle of your dreams from one of the premier manufacturers we work with.

Do you offer a warranty?

We don't offer a warranty on new bikes, but most manufacturers include a warranty on the bikes they make. We do offer a 90-day warranty on our repairs.

What kind of bike is right for me?

Our staff will help you choose which bike is right for you. Also, bikeradar has a fantastic article for beginners looking to know which type of bike suits them.
